## Interpretation Notes

- Unemployment Rate (%) indicates the percentage of the labor force that is unemployed.
- GDP Growth (%) indicates the annual growth rate of the economy.
- Inflation (%) indicates the annual percentage change in consumer prices.

## Agent Usage Guide

Agents should use this data as factual background when discussing employment conditions, economic anxiety, hiring expectations, consumer sentiment, and policy responses.

This dataset can help agents generate more grounded reactions during simulation.

## Simulation Hint

- Lower GDP growth and higher unemployment may indicate increased job market competition.
- Higher inflation may reduce real income and increase economic anxiety among agents.
